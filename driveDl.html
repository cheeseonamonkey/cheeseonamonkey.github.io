Google Drive OAuth

    How to Use

    This tool is designed to help you easily download the most recent releases of our app builds from Google Drive. Here's how to use it:

    1. Click on the "Authenticate Google Drive" link to grant the tool access to your Google Drive. You will be redirected to Google's authentication page.
    2. After successful authentication, you will be redirected back to this page. You will see a list of all your Google Drive folders.
    3. Use the search bar to filter the folders by name.
    4. Click on the checkbox next to a folder to pin it to the top of the list and to display the most recent files in the folder.
    5. Click on the "Download" link next to a file to download it.

    Troubleshooting

    If you encounter any issues, try the following steps:

    1. Ensure you are properly authenticated with Google Drive. If you are not, you will see the "Authenticate Google Drive" link.
    2. Refresh the page to reload the list of folders.
    3. Ensure the folder contains the files you are looking for.

    If the problem persists, please contact the support team.
